Retry Management

About the Retry Queue

When a tweet download fails, the system automatically schedules a retry. Retries use exponential backoff: 1st retry waits 2 min, 2nd waits 4 min, 3rd waits 8 min, up to a maximum of 60 min.

Actions

Retry Now: Force an immediate retry, ignoring the scheduled wait time.
Reset: Reset the retry counter and requeue the task.
Delete: Permanently remove this task.
